Property: deltaHeight (default is 20 pixels)
This property specifies by how much height needs to be increased or decreased.
Demo: default behaviour
Sample Code:
$( "#x-demo-1" ).quickpost({
	url: "assets/cricketers.json",
	valueProperty: "name",
	itemTemplate: "<div title='{@:email}'><div>{@:name}</div></div>",
	filterJSON: {startsWith:true}
});
Demo: Setting deltaHeight to 40
Sample Code:
$( "#x-demo-2" ).quickpost({
	url: "assets/cricketers.json",
	valueProperty: "name",
	itemTemplate: "<div title='{@:email}'><div>{@:name}</div></div>",
	filterJSON: {startsWith:true},
	deltaHeight: 40
});
